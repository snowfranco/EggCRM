# Billing & Plan Changes Troubleshooting

## Changing your plan
Upgrade or downgrade under **Settings > Billing > Plan**.
- **Upgrades take effect immediately.** You are charged a prorated amount for the remainder of the
  current billing cycle, and gain the new tier's features right away.
- **Downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle.** You keep your current
  features until then; no proration is owed for a downgrade.

## Switching monthly ↔ annual
Switch billing cadence at renewal under **Settings > Billing**. Annual billing receives a **20%
discount** off the monthly price and is paid up front for the year.

## Understanding a charge
To explain a charge, check the current plan, seat count, and billing cycle on the account, then
match it to the proration rules above. A mid-cycle upgrade produces a prorated line item; a seat
added mid-cycle is likewise prorated.

## Refunds
Refunds are available **within 14 days of the initial purchase only**, and are pro-rated. **A
support agent cannot approve refunds** — refund requests must be **escalated to the billing team**.
Do not promise a refund amount or approval; collect the details and hand off.

## Failed or overdue payment
If a payment fails, EggCRM retries the charge and emails the account's billing contact. While a
payment is overdue the account stays active for a grace period; persistent failure can suspend
write access. Have the customer update the card under **Settings > Billing > Payment Method**.
Payment **disputes** (not just a card update) go to the **billing team**.

## Cancellation
Cancellation requires **30 days' notice**. **A support agent cannot process a cancellation
directly** — cancellation requests must be **escalated to the retention team**. The same applies
to a downgrade that carries churn risk.

## SLA credits (Enterprise)
The 99.9% uptime SLA applies to **Enterprise only**. To claim an SLA credit for an incident, the
customer must file a support ticket **within 5 business days** of the incident; see **Service
Levels & Support**.
