# Getting Started with EggCRM

## What EggCRM is
EggCRM is a cloud-based CRM platform for small and mid-sized businesses. It centralizes
contacts, deals, and customer communication, and adds automation, reporting, and
integrations as you move up the plan tiers. EggCRM is browser-based — there is nothing to
install — and your data is hosted in EggCRM's cloud.

## Creating your account
Sign up at the EggCRM website with a work email. New accounts start on a 14-day trial of the
Professional plan; at the end of the trial you choose a plan (Starter, Professional, or
Enterprise) or your account moves to Starter. Billing only begins when you select a paid plan.

## The three plans at a glance
- **Starter** — contact management, email integration, basic reporting, 5GB storage, email
  support. Best for very small teams getting organized.
- **Professional** — everything in Starter plus pipeline management, workflow automation, API
  access, Slack and Gmail integrations, 50GB storage, and priority email + chat support.
- **Enterprise** — everything in Professional plus Zapier, custom webhooks, SSO/SAML, advanced
  analytics, custom integrations, unlimited storage, a dedicated account manager, phone support,
  and a 99.9% uptime SLA.

See **Plans & Pricing** for prices and the full feature-by-tier matrix.

## First steps after sign-up
1. Invite your team from **Settings > Team > Invite Members** (each invited member uses one seat).
2. Import your contacts under **Contacts > Import** (CSV upload).
3. Connect your email under **Settings > Integrations** so messages sync to contact timelines.
4. On Professional or higher, set up your first pipeline and automation.

## Where to get help
In-app help is available on every plan. Support channels depend on your tier: Starter is email
support, Professional adds live chat with priority response, and Enterprise adds phone support
and a dedicated account manager. See **Service Levels & Support** for details.
