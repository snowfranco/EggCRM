# Plans & Pricing

## Plan prices
All plans are priced per user, per month. Annual billing receives a 20% discount off the
monthly price.

- **Starter** — $29/user/month, or $23.20/user/month billed annually.
- **Professional** — $79/user/month, or $63.20/user/month billed annually.
- **Enterprise** — $149/user/month, or $119.20/user/month billed annually.

## Billing cycles
EggCRM bills monthly or annually. Annual billing is paid up front for the year and receives the
20% discount. You can switch between monthly and annual at any renewal.

## What each plan includes
- **Starter:** contact management, email integration, basic reporting, 5GB storage, email
  support only.
- **Professional:** everything in Starter, plus pipeline management, workflow automation, API
  access, Slack and Gmail integrations, 50GB storage, and priority email + chat support.
- **Enterprise:** everything in Professional, plus Zapier integration, custom webhooks, SSO/SAML,
  advanced analytics, custom integrations, unlimited storage, a dedicated account manager, phone
  support, and a 99.9% uptime SLA.

## Feature availability by tier (reference)
- **Available on every plan (including Starter):** contact management, email integration, basic
  reporting.
- **Requires Professional or higher:** pipeline management, workflow automation, API access,
  Slack integration, Gmail integration, 50GB storage, priority email + chat support.
- **Enterprise-only:** Zapier integration, custom webhooks, SSO/SAML, advanced analytics, custom
  integrations, unlimited storage, dedicated account manager, phone support, 99.9% SLA.

So Zapier, custom webhooks, SSO/SAML, advanced analytics, and phone support are Enterprise-only;
Slack, Gmail, API access, and workflow automation require Professional or higher; contact
management and email integration are available on every plan including Starter.

## Changing plans
Plan **upgrades take effect immediately**, with a prorated charge for the remainder of the
current billing cycle. Plan **downgrades take effect at the start of the next billing cycle** —
you keep your current features until then. See **Billing & Plan Changes** for the step-by-step
and for refund and cancellation policy.
