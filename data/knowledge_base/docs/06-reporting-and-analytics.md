# Reporting & Analytics

EggCRM has two reporting layers: **basic reporting**, available on every plan, and **advanced
analytics**, which is Enterprise-only.

## Basic reporting (all plans)
Basic reporting lives under **Reports**. It includes prebuilt reports for contacts added over
time, deals by stage, deal win/loss, activity volume per team member, and a basic sales forecast
(weighted pipeline value by close date, available once you have pipelines on Professional+).
Reports can be filtered by date range, owner, and tag, and exported to CSV.

## Advanced analytics (Enterprise only)
Advanced analytics adds custom report builders, cohort and funnel analysis, multi-pipeline
revenue dashboards, goal tracking, and scheduled report delivery by email. Advanced analytics is
available **only on the Enterprise plan**. On Starter and Professional you will see basic reports
only; the advanced dashboards are hidden.

## Dashboards
Assemble report widgets into dashboards under **Reports > Dashboards**. Every plan can build
dashboards from the basic reports available to it; Enterprise dashboards can also include
advanced-analytics widgets.

## Exporting report data
Any report can be exported to CSV from the report's **Export** button. This is distinct from a
full-account data export — for a complete export of all your records, see **Data Export &
Management**.

## A note on dashboard performance
Dashboards that aggregate large data sets can load slowly during peak hours
(approximately 9–11 AM ET). This is a known issue being addressed in an upcoming release. See
**Dashboard Performance** for current guidance and workarounds.
