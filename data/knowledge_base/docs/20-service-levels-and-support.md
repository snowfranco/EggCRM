# Service Levels & Support

## Support channels by tier
- **Starter:** email support only.
- **Professional:** priority email **and** live chat support.
- **Enterprise:** priority email, live chat, **phone support**, and a **dedicated account
  manager**.

Phone support and the dedicated account manager are **Enterprise-only**.

## Uptime SLA (Enterprise only)
A **99.9% uptime SLA** is available **only on the Enterprise plan**. Starter and Professional do
not carry a contractual uptime guarantee.

## Claiming an SLA credit
If an Enterprise account experiences downtime that breaches the SLA, the customer may be eligible
for an SLA credit. To claim it, they must **file a support ticket within 5 business days** of the
incident. Credits are reviewed against measured uptime for the affected period.

## Ticket priorities
When support files a ticket, priority is set by impact:
- **Urgent** — service down or data loss affecting many users.
- **High** — major feature broken, no workaround.
- **Normal** — a feature is impaired but has a workaround.
- **Low** — minor issue, question, or cosmetic problem.

## What gets escalated, and to whom
Some requests are beyond what a support agent can resolve directly and are routed to a specialist
team:
- **Billing team** — refund requests, billing disputes, payment failures.
- **Retention team** — cancellation requests, and downgrades with churn risk.
- **Integrations team** — SSO/SAML configuration and complex API issues.
- **Compliance team** — GDPR requests, legal inquiries, data-processing agreements.
- **Engineering** — confirmed bugs with reproduction steps.
- **Supervisor / management** — abusive situations or threats of legal action.

Agents cannot approve refunds, process cancellations, or handle GDPR deletions directly — these
are always escalated to the team above.

## Status page and incidents
Before troubleshooting a widespread problem, check the EggCRM **status page** for active
incidents. Subscribed users receive incident notifications there.
